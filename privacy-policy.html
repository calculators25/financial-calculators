Your Privacy Matters: Financial Calculators is committed to protecting your privacy. All calculations are performed locally in your browser, and we do not collect, store, or share your personal financial information.

1. Information We Collect

Calculator Data

When you use our financial calculators:

  • All calculations are performed in your browser - Your financial data never leaves your device
  • We do not store any numbers, amounts, or personal financial information you enter
  • Calculator inputs are not transmitted to our servers

Website Analytics

We may collect non-personal information through analytics services:

  • Pages visited and time spent on site
  • Browser type and device information
  • General geographic location (country/city level)
  • Referring website

Cookies

Our website may use cookies for:

  • Website functionality and user preferences
  • Analytics to improve user experience
  • Advertising (Google AdSense cookies)

2. How We Use Information

Any non-personal information collected is used to:

  • Improve our calculators and website functionality
  • Understand which calculators are most useful to visitors
  • Optimize website performance
  • Display relevant advertisements

3. Third-Party Services

Google AdSense

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website or other websites. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ads Settings.

Analytics

We may use analytics services like Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our site. These services may collect information such as your IP address, browser type, and pages visited.

4. Your Financial Data Privacy

Important: We want to be absolutely clear:
  • We NEVER see the numbers you enter into calculators
  • We NEVER store your calculation results
  • We NEVER share your financial information (because we don't have it!)
  • All calculator functions run entirely in your browser using JavaScript

5. Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Disable cookies in your browser settings
  • Opt out of personalized advertising
  • Request information about data collected through analytics
  • Use our calculators without providing any personal information

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

8. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please visit our Contact Page.

Our website may contain links to external websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of other websites. We encourage you to read the privacy policies of any linked websites you visit.

10. Data Security

Since all calculations occur in your browser and we don't store your financial data, there is no risk of your calculation data being compromised through our servers. However, we implement reasonable security measures for our website infrastructure.
