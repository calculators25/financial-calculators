üí° Refinancing Tips

  • Shop around: Get quotes from at least 3 lenders - rates can vary by 0.5% or more
  • Check your credit score: 740+ gets best rates. Improve score before applying
  • Consider no-closing-cost refinance: Higher rate but no upfront fees
  • Don't reset the clock: If you have 20 years left, refi to 20, not 30 years
  • Rule of thumb: Refinance if you can lower rate by 0.75% or more
  • Break-even matters: Only worth it if you'll stay in home past break-even point
  • Cash-out carefully: Taking cash out increases debt - use for investments/renovations only
  • Lock your rate: Rates change daily - lock when you find a good one
