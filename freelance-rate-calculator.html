üí° Pricing Tips for Freelancers

  • Don't underprice: Your rate should cover ALL costs plus profit. Remember, no paid vacation or benefits!
  • Not all hours are billable: Account for admin work, marketing, and unpaid project time
  • Raise rates regularly: Increase 10-15% annually or when you gain new skills
  • Value-based pricing: For experienced freelancers, charge based on value delivered, not just hours
  • Have a minimum project rate: Small projects should have minimums (e.g., $500-1000)
  • Factor in payment delays: Clients may take 30-60 days to pay - plan cash flow accordingly
  • Premium for rush jobs: Charge 50-100% more for urgent requests
  • Retainer discounts: Offer 10-15% off for monthly retainers (guaranteed income!)
