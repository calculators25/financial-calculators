Frequently Asked Questions

Is this website really free?

Yes! All our calculators are 100% free to use with no signup required. We're supported by advertising.

Do you store my financial information?

No. All calculations happen in your browser using JavaScript. We never see, store, or transmit the numbers you enter. See our Privacy Policy for details.

Are the calculator results accurate?

Our calculators use standard financial formulas and are designed to provide accurate estimates. However, results are for educational purposes only. Always consult with a qualified financial advisor for personalized advice.

Can I use these calculators on my phone?

Yes! All our calculators are mobile-friendly and work on smartphones, tablets, and desktop computers.

Why do I see ads on the website?

Advertising helps us keep the calculators free for everyone. We use Google AdSense to display relevant ads.

Can I embed your calculators on my website?

Currently, we don't offer embeddable widgets. However, you're welcome to link to our calculators. For partnership inquiries, please contact us.

How often are calculators updated?

We regularly review and update our calculators to ensure accuracy and add new features based on user feedback.

Do you offer financial advice?

No. We provide calculators and tools only. We are not financial advisors. Always consult with a qualified professional for personalized financial advice.
