üí° Student Loan Repayment Tips

  • Public Service Loan Forgiveness (PSLF): Working for government/non-profit? Make 120 payments and get remaining balance forgiven
  • Pay more than minimum: Extra payments go directly to principal - can save thousands in interest
  • Refinance if rates drop: Private refinancing can lower rates but you lose federal protections
  • Autopay discount: Most lenders offer 0.25% rate reduction for automatic payments
  • Income-Driven plans: If struggling, IBR/PAYE/REPAYE base payments on income (10-15% of discretionary income)
  • Don't ignore your loans: Default damages credit and can result in wage garnishment
  • Employer assistance: Some employers offer student loan repayment benefits - ask HR!
  • Target high-interest loans first: If you have multiple loans, pay minimums on all, extra on highest rate
